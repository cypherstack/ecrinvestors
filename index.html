What is EPICENTER?

Epicenter is a community-driven, privacy-preserving ecosystem composed of independent interoperable “money legos” that work seamlessly with each other to bring down barriers to entry and friction that prevent universal access and adoption. They also interconnect with other blockchains.

Our starting point was $EPIC, the native currency of the EPIC Blockchain Protocol, and the key component to make it all work. Epic Private Internet Cash - “Epic Cash” or simply “EPIC” is what Bitcoin would be if Satoshi were developing it in 2019 based on available tech at that time plus the experience gained from the 1000s of altcoin experiments till date.

Epic is a lightweight, scalable, privacy-enabled mobile-native blockchain that is faster, more agile, more flexible, future-proofed and extensible than today’s ossified, frozen in 2008 BTC with its prevailing ‘HODL’ ideology and aversion to base layer innovation.

Clearly, what is needed is a type of asset that will tend toward $1 in value, but not have any connection at all to anything that any government can attack, seeing as how even mighty USDC just got a SEC subpoena. For this, we creatively reference $EPIC in a pre-1971 dollar-inspired tending-towards-stable floor-pegged cryptodollar design known as EUSD. Censorship resistance is paramount, and regulatory compatibility is a central reason for that.

$EPIC plays the role of gold in our Exter’s Pyramid, as it is the exclusive collateral underpinning the EUSD cryptodollar token. EUSD is, like the pre-1971, Federal Reserve Note, redeemable on demand for real money - in the case of the Federal Reserve Note, 1/35th oz of physical gold (only open to central banks, and only if the government allowed it, of course).

With EUSD, it’s $1 worth of $EPIC zapped directly to the user’s wallet via anonymous smart contract where 24/7, anyone, anytime, and nobody can ever be denied service. This cryptodollar is, like its namesake the Eurodollar, a dollar that exists outside the scope of the traditional system.

Anyone, anywhere can create EUSD by interacting with a smart contract on one of 30+ different free open source blockchains (Ethereum and its competition). The minting is controlled by an algorithmic central bank represented by ECR tokenholders and they must own $EPIC to do so. It is a fully-reserved, autonomous, infinite, decentralized self-banking system.

For $EPIC holders, EUSD on-demand liquidity represents a convenient and low-cost source of capital with a range of available equity-based lines of credit available.
